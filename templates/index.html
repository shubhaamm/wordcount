The Lakehead-Georgian advantage

Studying at Lakehead-Georgian isn’t a typical experience – it’s a one-of-a-kind education!

  • Experience the best of university and college education in many integrated programs, earning both a university degree and college diploma in four years.
  • Be an innovator who combines academic scholarship, applied skills and critical reasoning to contribute to workplaces and communities.

Get $4,000 toward first year

All domestic students are eligible for the Lakehead-Georgian Entrance Award. The $4,000 award can be combined with a Lakehead Entrance Scholarship to a maximum of free tuition.
